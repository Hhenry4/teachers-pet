Teacher's Pet

The definitive assignment dashboard built natively on the Google Classroom API.

How It Works

Teacher's Pet connects securely to your Google Account leveraging the official, enterprise-grade Google Classroom API. It pulls your coursework into an ultra-fast, visually premium ecosystem designed exclusively for productivity. Your data never leaves the Google loop—meaning it's 100% secure.

📚

Premium Organization

Say goodbye to cluttered streams. We elegantly sort your assignments by course, automatically labeling high-priority and past-due work so you can zero in instantly.

🎯

Prioritized Study Routine

Overwhelmed by work? Our intelligent study algorithm mathematically constructs a day-by-day roadmap, prioritizing your closest deadlines so you know exactly what to tackle first.

🔔

Background Notifications

Backed by a custom 24/7 Node.js server, Teacher's Pet constantly polls the Classroom API and fires native desktop push notifications the second a teacher posts something new.
